🔒 Privacy Policy

Small Brain Studio ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Liquid Sort mobile game ("the Game"). We encourage you to read this policy carefully so that you understand our practices.

📅 Last updated: May 2025
1
Information We Collect

Liquid Sort does not directly collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. However, our third-party advertising partner Google AdMob may automatically collect certain data to serve relevant advertisements. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / GAID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • Ad performance and engagement metrics
ℹ️ We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details directly through the Game.
2
How We Use Information

Any data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) is used solely to:

  • Display relevant and personalized advertisements within the Game
  • Measure ad performance and improve the advertising experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

3
Third-Party Services

The Game integrates Google AdMob for in-app advertising. AdMob is operated by Google LLC and has its own data collection and privacy practices. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

📄 Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
📄 AdMob's Terms: https://developers.google.com/admob/terms
4
Data Storage

All game progress data — including your score, completed levels, and sound settings — is stored locally on your device only using the device's built-in secure storage. We do not upload, sync, or backup your game data to any external servers or cloud services.

🗑️ Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored game data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Liquid Sort is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The Game does not contain content specifically targeting children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately at smallbrainstudio@outlook.com and we will take prompt action to remove such information.

7
Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device's advertising settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID at any time through device settings
  • Request information about data collected by third-party services
  • Contact us to request data deletion (local data can be cleared by uninstalling)
📱 Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
🍎 iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable tracking
8
Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Since all game data is stored locally on your device, we rely on your device's built-in security features (e.g., device encryption, OS-level sandboxing) to safeguard this data.

No method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
